SMUS IFF Simple Musical Score / Standard Data and Property Chunks ]" border=0> A FORM SMUS contains a required property " SHDR " followed by any number of parallel "track" data chunks " TRAK ". Optional property chunks such as "NAME", copyright "(c) ", and instrument reference "INS1" may also appear. Any of the properties may be shared over a LIST of FORMs SMUS by putting them in a PROP SMUS .
