TITLE: Amiga® RKM Devices: 12 Serial Device

Amiga® RKM Devices: 12 Serial Device ]" border=0> The serial device provides a hardware-independent interface to the Amiga's built-in RS-232C compatible serial port. Serial ports have a wide range of uses, including communication with modems, printers, MIDI devices, and other computers. The same device interface can be used for additional "byte stream oriented devices" - usually more serial ports. The serial device is based on the conventions of Exec device I/O, with extensions for parameter setting and control.
