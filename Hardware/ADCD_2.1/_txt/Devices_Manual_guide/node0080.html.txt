TITLE: Amiga® RKM Devices: 4 Console Device

Amiga® RKM Devices: 4 Console Device ]" border=0> The console device provides the text-oriented interface for Intuition windows. It acts like an enhanced ASCII terminal obeying many of the standard ANSI sequences as well as special sequences unique to the Amiga. The console device also provides a copy-and-paste facility and an internal character map to redraw a window when it is resized.
