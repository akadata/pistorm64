TITLE: Standard Data and Property Chunks / Future Properties

Standard Data and Property Chunks / Future Properties ]" border=0> New optional property chunks may be defined in the future to store additional formatting information. They will be used to represent formatting not encoded in standard ISO/ANSI control sequences and for "inherited" formatting in structured documents. Text orientation might be one example.
