TITLE: Composite FORMs / ILBM

Composite FORMs / ILBM ]" border=0> " ILBM " is an InterLeaved BitMap image with color map; a machine-independent format for raster images. FORM ILBM is the standard image file format for the Commodore-Amiga computer and is useful in other environments, too. See the supplemental document "ILBM" IFF Interleaved Bitmap.
