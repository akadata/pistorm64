3 / Device Interface / Closing The Clipboard Device ]" border=0> Each OpenDevice() must eventually be matched by a call to CloseDevice() . CloseDevice(ClipIO); When the last task closes a clipboard unit with CloseDevice(), the contents of the unit may be copied to a disk file in CLIPS: so that the clipboard device can be expunged.
