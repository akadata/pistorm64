TITLE: Amiga® RKM Devices: 8 Narrator Device

Amiga® RKM Devices: 8 Narrator Device ]" border=0> This chapter describes the narrator device which, together with the translator library, provides all of the Amiga's text-to-speech functions. The narrator device is used to produce high-quality human-like speech in real time.
