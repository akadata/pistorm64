This chunk is required and should only appear once in a document in the outermost layer of the DR2D file (DR2Ds can be nested). struct DRHDstruct { ULONG ID; ULONG Size; /* Always 16 */ IEEE XLeft, YTop, XRight, YBot; }; The point (XLeft,YTop) is the upper left corner of the project and the point (XRight,YBot) is its lower right corner.
