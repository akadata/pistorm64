clipboard.device/CBD_CHANGEHOOK ]" border=0> NAME CBD_CHANGEHOOK -- Add or remove a clip change hook FUNCTION CBD_CHANGEHOOK allows specification of a hook to be called when the data on the clipboard has changed.
