TITLE: iffparse.library/FreeIFF

iffparse.library/FreeIFF ]" border=0> NAME FreeIFF -- deallocate an IFFHandle structure. (V36) SYNOPSIS FreeIFF(iff); A0 VOID FreeIFF(struct IFFHandle *); FUNCTION Deallocates all resources associated with this IFFHandle structure. The structure MUST have already been closed with CloseIFF() . INPUTS iff - pointer to IFFHandle structure to free. Starting with V39, this may be NULL.
