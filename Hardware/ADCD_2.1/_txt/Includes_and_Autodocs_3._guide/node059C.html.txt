(V36) SYNOPSIS seconds = Date2Amiga(date); D0 A0 ULONG Date2Amiga(struct ClockData *); FUNCTION Calculates the number of seconds from 01-Jan-1978 to the date specified in the ClockData structure. INPUTS date - pointer to a ClockData structure containing the date of interest. RESULTS seconds - the number of seconds from 01-Jan-1978 to the date specified. WARNING This function does no sanity checking of the data in the ClockData structure. SEE ALSO Amiga2Date() , CheckDate() [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
