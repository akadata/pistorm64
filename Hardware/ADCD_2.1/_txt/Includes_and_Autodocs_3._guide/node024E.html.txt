TITLE: SAD/NOP

SAD/NOP ]" border=0> NO-OP - Do nothing other than tell SAD you are still there... Command: $AF $00 Data: This just tells SAD you are still there. Required so that timeouts do not exit SAD while you are not doing anything. This command will *NOT* be ACK'ed. It will just cause the timeout to be restarted. [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
