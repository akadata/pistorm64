intuition.library/RefreshWindowFrame ]" border=0> NAME RefreshWindowFrame -- Ask Intuition to redraw your window border. SYNOPSIS RefreshWindowFrame( Window ) A0 VOID RefreshWindowFrame( struct Window * ); FUNCTION Refreshes the border of a window, including title region and all of the window's gadgets. You may use this call if you wish to update the display of your borders. The expected use of this is to correct unavoidable corruption.
