graphics.library/FreeBitMap ]" border=0> NAME FreeBitMap -- free a bitmap created by AllocBitMap (V39) SYNOPSIS FreeBitMap(bm) a0 VOID FreeBitMap(struct BitMap *) FUNCTION Frees bitmap and all associated bitplanes INPUTS bm = A pointer to a BitMap structure.
