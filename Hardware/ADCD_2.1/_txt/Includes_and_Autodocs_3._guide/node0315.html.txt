graphics.library/RemBob ]" border=0> NAME RemBob -- Macro to remove a Bob from the gel list. SYNOPSIS RemBob(bob) RemBob(struct Bob *); FUNCTION Marks a Bob as no-longer-required. The gels internal code then removes the Bob from the list of active gels the next time DrawGList is executed. This is implemented as a macro. If the user is double-buffering the Bob , it could take two calls to DrawGList before the Bob actually disappears from the RastPort .
