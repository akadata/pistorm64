TITLE: serial.device/CMD_STOP

serial.device/CMD_STOP ]" border=0> NAME Stop -- pause all current I/O over the serial port FUNCTION This command halts all current I/O on the serial port by sending an xOFF to the "other side", and submitting a "logical xOFF" to "our side", if/when appropriate to current activity.
