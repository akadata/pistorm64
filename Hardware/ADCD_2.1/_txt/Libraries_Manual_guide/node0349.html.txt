27 Graphics Primitives / Drawing Routines ]" border=0> Most of the graphics drawing routines require information about how the drawing is to take place. For this reason, most graphics drawing routines use a data structure called a RastPort , that contains pointers to the drawing area and drawing variables such as the current pen color and font to use. In general, you pass a pointer to your RastPort structure as an argument whenever you call a drawing function.
