TITLE: Amiga® RKM Libraries: 10 Intuition Mouse and Keyboard

Amiga® RKM Libraries: 10 Intuition Mouse and Keyboard ]" border=0> In the Intuition system, the mouse is the normal method of making selections and the keyboard is used for entering character data. This section describes how users employ the mouse to interact with the system and how to arrange for a program to use the mouse. It also describes the use of the keyboard, both as a character input device and as an alternate method of controlling the mouse pointer.
