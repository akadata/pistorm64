TITLE: Amiga® RKM Libraries: 17 Introduction to Exec

Amiga® RKM Libraries: 17 Introduction to Exec ]" border=0> The Multitasking Executive, better known as Exec, is the heart of the Amiga's operating system. All other systems in the Amiga rely on it to control multitasking, to manage the message-based interprocess communications system, and to arbitrate access to system resources. Because just about every software entity on the Amiga (including application programs) needs to use Exec in some way, every Amiga programmer has to have a basic understanding of its fundamentals.
