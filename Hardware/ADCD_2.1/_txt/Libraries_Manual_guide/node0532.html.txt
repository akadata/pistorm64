TITLE: B / groupgclass / Changed: GM_GOACTIVE/GM_GOINACTIVE/GM_HANDLEINPUT

B / groupgclass / Changed: GM_GOACTIVE/GM_GOINACTIVE/GM_HANDLEINPUT ]" border=0> This method passes the message to the active member's dispatcher for processing. For GM_GOINACTIVE and GM_HANDLEINPUT, the coordinates passed to the member's dispatcher in the message's gpi_Mouse.X and gpi_Mouse.Y fields are translated so that they are relative to the gadget's upper-left corner.
