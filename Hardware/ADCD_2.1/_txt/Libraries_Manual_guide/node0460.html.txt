33 / / FORM ILBM / Sample Hex Dump of an ILBM ]" border=0> WARNING: -------- This hex dump is shown only to help the reader understand how IFF chunks appear in a file. You cannot ever depend on any particular ILBM chunk being at any particular offset into the file. IFF files are composed, in their simplest form, of chunks within a FORM . Each chunk starts with a 4-letter chunkID, followed by a 32-bit length of the rest of the chunk. You must parse IFF files, skipping past unneeded or unknown chunks by seeking their length (+1 if odd length) to the next 4-letter chunk ID. In a real ILBM file, you are likely to encounter additional optional chunks.
