5 / / Proportional Gadget Components / Using the Body and Pot Values ]" border=0> The body and pot values of a proportional gadget are "Intuition friendly" numbers, in that they represent concepts convenient to Intuition, and not to the application. The application must translate these numbers to internal values before acting on them. [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
