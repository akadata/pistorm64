33 / Context Functions / Context-Specific Data: LocalContextItems ]" border=0> ContextNode s can contain application data specific to that context. These data objects are called LocalContextItem s. LocalContextItems (LCIs) are a grey-box structure which contain a type, ID and identification field. LCIs are used to store context-sensitive data. The format of this data is application-defined. The IFFHandle is the root of the tree, so to speak. ContextNodes "grow" out of the IFFHandle. In turn, LCIs "grow" out of the ContextNodes. What grows out of an LCI is client-defined.
