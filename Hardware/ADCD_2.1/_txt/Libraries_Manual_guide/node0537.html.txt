D / Errors / Audio--Corrupted Samples ]" border=0> The bit data for audio samples must be in Chip RAM. Check your compiler manual for directives or flags which will place your audio sample data in Chip RAM. Or dynamically allocate Chip RAM and copy or load the audio sample there.
