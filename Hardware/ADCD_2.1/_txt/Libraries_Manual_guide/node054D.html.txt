TITLE: D / Errors / Graphics--Corrupted Images

D / Errors / Graphics--Corrupted Images ]" border=0> The bit data for graphic images such as sprites, pointers, bobs, and gadgets must be in Chip RAM. Check your compiler manual for directives or flags which will place your graphic image data in Chip RAM. Or dynamically allocate Chip RAM and copy them there. [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
