TITLE: Glossary / bus hogging

Glossary / bus hogging ]" border=0> When a bus master takes over the bus for an undue amount of time. The Zorro II bus leaves it completely up to the individual PIC to avoid bus hogging; the Zorro III bus schedules PICs with the bus controller to evenly distribute the bus load.
