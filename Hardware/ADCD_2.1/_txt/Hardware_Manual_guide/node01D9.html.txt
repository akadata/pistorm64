TITLE: Glossary / coprocessor

Glossary / coprocessor ]" border=0> An extra processor that enhances system performance by doing a specialized task, such as graphics or math, very quickly. This frees the main processor to do other work. Every Amiga has at least three coprocessor chips named Paula, Agnus, and Denise to handle graphics and audio. [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
