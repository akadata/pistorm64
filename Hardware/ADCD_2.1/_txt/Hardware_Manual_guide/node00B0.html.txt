4 Sprite Hardware / Forming a Sprite ]" border=0> To form a sprite, you must first define it and then create a formal data structure in memory. You define a sprite by specifying its characteristics: * On-screen width of up to 16 pixels. * Unlimited height. * Any shape. * A combination of three colors, plus transparent. * Any position on the screen.
