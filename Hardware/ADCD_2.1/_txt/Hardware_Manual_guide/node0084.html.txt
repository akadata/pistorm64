3 / / Picture is Larger than Window / Selecting the Stopping Position ]" border=0> The stopping position for the display window is the horizontal and vertical coordinates of the lower right-hand corner of the display window. One register, DIWSTOP , contains both coordinates, known as HSTOP and VSTOP.
