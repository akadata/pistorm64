POT1DAT 014 R P( E ) Pot counter data right pair (vert,horiz.) These addresses each read a pair of 8-bit pot counters. (Four counters total.) The bit assignment for both addresses is shown below. The counters are stopped by signals from two controller connectors (left-right) with two pins each.
