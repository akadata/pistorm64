TITLE: K / Electrical Specifications / Slot Power Availability

K / Electrical Specifications / Slot Power Availability ]" border=0> The system power for the Zorro III bus is totally based on the slot configurations. A backplane is always free to supply extra power, but it must meet the minimum requirements specified here. All PICs must be designed with the minimum specifications in mind, especially the tolerances.
