3 Playfield Hardware / Summary of Color Selection Registers ]" border=0> This section contains summaries of the playfield color selection registers including color register contents, example colors, and the differences in color selection in high resolution and low resolution modes. The Amiga has 32 color registers and each one has 4 bits of red, 4 bits of green, and 4 bits of blue information. Table 3-15 shows the bit assignments of each color register. All color registers are write-only.
