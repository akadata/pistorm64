TITLE: Glossary / Command Line Interface (Shell or CLI)

Glossary / Command Line Interface (Shell or CLI) ]" border=0> A means of communicating with a computer by typing commands at the keyboard. On the Amiga, this is called the Shell and, along with Workbench and ARexx, is one of the three built-in user interfaces. Before the Shell was available, this interface was called the CLI.
