TITLE: Glossary / backplane

Glossary / backplane ]" border=0> The cage or motherboard subsection into which PICs are inserted. The Amiga 2000 and Amiga 3000 computers have integral backplanes, the Amiga 500 and Amiga 1000 computers require add-on backplane cages for Zorro II compatibility. [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
