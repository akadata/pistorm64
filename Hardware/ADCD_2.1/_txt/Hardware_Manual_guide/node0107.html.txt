E / / Serial Interface Specification / Timing ]" border=0> Maximum operating frequency is 19.2 KHz. Refer to EIA standard RS-232-C for operating and installation specifications. A rate of 31.25 KHz will be supported through the use of a MIDI adapter. Modem control signals ( CTS, RTS, DTR, DSR, CD ) are completely under software control. The modem control lines have no hardware affect on and are completely asynchronous to TXD and RXD . [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
