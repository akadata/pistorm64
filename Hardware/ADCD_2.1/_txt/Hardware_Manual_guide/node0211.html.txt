TITLE: Glossary / parallel port

Glossary / parallel port ]" border=0> A connector on the back of the Amiga that allows extra equipment such as a printer to be attached. The parallel port transfers data one complete byte (8 bits) at a time, in contrast to the serial port which sends a single bit at a time. [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
