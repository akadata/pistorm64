7 / / Setting and Clearing Bits / Blitter Interrupt ]" border=0> Bit 6, BLIT, signals "blitter finished." If this bit is a 1, it indicates that the blitter has completed the requested data transfer. The blitter is now ready to accept another task. This bit generates a level 3 interrupt . [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
