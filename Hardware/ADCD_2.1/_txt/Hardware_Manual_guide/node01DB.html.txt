TITLE: Glossary / cycle strobe

Glossary / cycle strobe ]" border=0> A bus signal that defines the boundary of a bus cycle; the Zorro II and Zorro III modes on a Zorro III bus each have their own cycle strobes. The current bus master always asserts the cycle strobes. [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
