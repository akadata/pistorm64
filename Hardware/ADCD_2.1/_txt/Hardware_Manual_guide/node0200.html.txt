TITLE: Glossary / master

Glossary / master ]" border=0> The device currently generating addresses for the expansion bus. There is only one master on the bus at a time, this being insured by the bus arbitration logic. The master also drives data on writes, the read, cycle, and data strobes, and several other signals. [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
