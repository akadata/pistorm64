F / / Interface Signals / R/W - read/write input ]" border=0> The R/W signal is normally supplied by the microprocessor and controls the direction of data transfers of the 8520. A high on R/W indicates a read (data transfer out of the 8520), while a low indicates a write (data transfer into the 8520). [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
