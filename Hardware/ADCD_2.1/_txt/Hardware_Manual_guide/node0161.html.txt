TITLE: 7 / Interrupts / Nonmaskable Interrupt

7 / Interrupts / Nonmaskable Interrupt ]" border=0> Interrupt level 7 is the nonmaskable interrupt and is not generated anywhere in the current system. The raw interrupt lines of the 680x0, IPL2 through IPL0, are brought out to the expansion connector and can be used to generate this level 7 interrupt for debugging purposes.
