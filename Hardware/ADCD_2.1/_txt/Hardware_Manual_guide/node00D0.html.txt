4 Sprite Hardware / Summary of Sprite Color Registers ]" border=0> Sprite data words are used to select the color of the sprite pixels from the system color register set as indicated in the following tables. If the bit combinations from single sprites are as shown in Table 4-6, then the colors will be taken from the registers shown. Table 4-6: Color Registers for Single Sprites Single Sprites -------------- Sprite Value Color Register ------ ----- -------------- 0 or 1 00 Not used * 01 17 10 18 11 19 2 or 3 00 Not used * 01 21 10 22 11 23 4 or 5 00 Not used * 01 25 10 26 11 27 6 or 7 00 Not used * 01 29 10 30 11 31 * Selects transparent mode. If the bit combinations from attached sprites are as shown in Table 4-7, then the colors will be taken from the registers shown.
