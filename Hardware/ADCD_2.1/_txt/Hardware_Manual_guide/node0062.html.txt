3 Playfield Hardware / About Amiga Playfields ]" border=0> A playfield forms the basic foundation of an Amiga display and determines its fundamental characteristics. To form a playfield, you program the hardware registers of the custom chips with the basic parameters of the type of display you want. Forming a playfield involves selecting the number of colors, setting up a color table and bitplanes, and selecting the resolution and display mode.
