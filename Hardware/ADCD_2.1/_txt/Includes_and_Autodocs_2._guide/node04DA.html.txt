TITLE: clipboard.device/CBD_CURRENTREADID

clipboard.device/CBD_CURRENTREADID ]" border=0> NAME CBD_CURRENTREADID - Determine the current read identifier. FUNCTION CBD_CURRENTREADID fills the io_ClipID with a clip identifier that can be compared with that of a post command: if greater than the post identifier then the post data held privately by an application is not valid for its own pasting.
