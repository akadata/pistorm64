clipboard.device/CMD_UPDATE ]" border=0> NAME CMD_UPDATE -- Terminate the writing of a clip to the clipboard. FUNCTION Indicate to the clipboard that the previous write commands are complete and can be used for any pending pastes (reads). This command cannot be issued while any of the write commands are pending.
