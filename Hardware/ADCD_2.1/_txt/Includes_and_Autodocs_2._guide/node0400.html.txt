mathieeedoubbas.library/IEEEDPFloor ]" border=0> NAME IEEEDPFloor -- compute Floor function of IEEE double precision number SYNOPSIS x = IEEEDPFloor( y ); d0/d1 d0/d1 double x,y; FUNCTION Calculate the largest integer less than or equal to x and return it.
