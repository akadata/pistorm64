dos.library/SendPkt ]" border=0> NAME SendPkt -- Sends a packet to a handler (V36) SYNOPSIS SendPkt(packet, port, replyport) D1 D2 D3 void SendPkt(struct DosPacket *,struct MsgPort *,struct MsgPort *) FUNCTION Sends a packet to a handler and does not wait. All fields in the packet must be initialized before calling this routine. The packet will be returned to replyport. If you wish to use this with WaitPkt() , use the address or your pr_MsgPort for replyport. INPUTS packet - packet to send, must be initialized and have a message. port - pr_MsgPort of handler process to send to. replyport - MsgPort for the packet to come back to. NOTES Callable from a task. SEE ALSO DoPkt() , WaitPkt() , AllocDosObject() , FreeDosObject() , AbortPkt() [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
