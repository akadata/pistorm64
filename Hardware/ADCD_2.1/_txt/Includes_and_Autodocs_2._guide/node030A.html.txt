TITLE: dos.library/UnLockRecords

dos.library/UnLockRecords ]" border=0> NAME UnLockRecords -- Unlock a list of records (V36) SYNOPSIS success = UnLockRecords(record_array) D0 D1 BOOL UnLockRecords(struct RecordLock *) FUNCTION This releases an array of record locks obtained using LockRecords . You should NOT modify the record_array while you have the records locked. Every LockRecords() call must be balanced with an UnLockRecords() call. INPUTS record_array - List of records to be unlocked RESULT success - Success or failure. BUGS See LockRecord() SEE ALSO LockRecords() , LockRecord() , UnLockRecord() [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
