TITLE: iffparse.library/InitIFFasClip

iffparse.library/InitIFFasClip ]" border=0> NAME InitIFFasClip -- Initialize an IFFHandle as a clipboard stream. SYNOPSIS InitIFFasClip (iff) a0 struct IFFHandle *iff; FUNCTION Initializes the given IFFHandle to be a clipboard stream. The function initializes the stream processing vectors to operate on streams of the ClipboardHandle type. The iff_Stream field will still need to be initialized to point to a ClipboardHandle as returned from OpenClipboard() . INPUTS iff - pointer to IFFHandle struct.
