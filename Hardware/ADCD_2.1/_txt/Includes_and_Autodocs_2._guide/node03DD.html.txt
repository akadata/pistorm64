utility.library/SDivMod32 ]" border=0> NAME SDivMod32 -- Signed 32 by 32 bit division and modulus. (V36) SYNOPSIS Quotient:Remainder = SDivMod32( Dividend, Divisor ) D0 D1 D0 D1 LONG SDivMod32( LONG, LONG ); FUNCTION Divides the signed 32 bit dividend by the signed 32 bit divisor and returns a signed 32 bit quotient and remainder. INPUTS Dividend - signed 32 bit dividend. Divisor - signed 32 bit divisor. RESULTS Quotient - signed 32 quotient of the division. Remainder - signed 32 remainder of the division.
