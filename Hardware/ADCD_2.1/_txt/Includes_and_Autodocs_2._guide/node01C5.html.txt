iffparse.library/FreeIFF ]" border=0> NAME FreeIFF -- Deallocate an IFFHandle struct. SYNOPSIS FreeIFF (iff) a0 struct IFFHandle *iff; FUNCTION Deallocates all resources associated with this IFFHandle struct. The struct MUST have already been closed with CloseIFF() .
