TITLE: commodities.library/SetCxObjPri

commodities.library/SetCxObjPri ]" border=0> NAME SetCxObjPri -- set the priority of a commodity object. (V36) SYNOPSIS SetCxObjPri(co,pri) A0 D0 VOID SetCxObjPri(CxObj *,LONG); FUNCTION This function sets the priority of a commodity object for the purposes of EnqueueCxObj() . It is strongly recommended that the ToolTypes environment be utilized to provide end-user control over the priority of brokers, but application specific ordering of other objects within their lists is not dictated. INPUTS co - the commodity object to affect pri - the object's new priority in the range -128 to +127. A value of 0 is normal. SEE ALSO EnqueueCxObj() [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
