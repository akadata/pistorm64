TITLE: utility.library/UDivMod32

utility.library/UDivMod32 ]" border=0> NAME UDivMod32 -- Unsigned 32 by 32 bit division and modulus. (V36) SYNOPSIS Quotient:Remainder = UDivMod32( Dividend, Divisor ) D0 D1 D0 D1 ULONG UDivMod32( ULONG, ULONG ); FUNCTION Divides the unsigned 32 bit dividend by the unsigned 32 bit divisor and returns a unsigned 32 bit quotient and remainder. INPUTS Dividend - unsigned 32 bit dividend. Divisor - unsigned 32 bit divisor. RESULTS Quotient - unsigned 32 quotient of the division. Remainder - unsigned 32 remainder of the division.
