iffparse.library/InitIFFasDOS ]" border=0> NAME InitIFFasDOS -- Initialize an IFFHandle as a DOS stream. SYNOPSIS InitIFFasDOS (iff) a0 struct IFFHandle *iff; FUNCTION The function initializes the given IFFHandle to operate on DOS streams. The iff_Stream field will need to be initialized as a BPTR returned from the DOS function Open() . INPUTS iff - pointer to IFFHandle struct.
