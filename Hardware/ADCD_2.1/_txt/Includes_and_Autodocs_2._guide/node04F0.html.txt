TITLE: parallel.device/CMD_STOP

parallel.device/CMD_STOP ]" border=0> NAME Stop -- pause current activity on the parallel device FUNCTION This command halts the current I/O activity on the parallel device by discontinuing the handshaking sequence. The stop and start commands may not be nested.
