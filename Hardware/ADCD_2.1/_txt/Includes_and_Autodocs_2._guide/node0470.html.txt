graphics.library/RemIBob ]" border=0> NAME RemIBob -- Immediately remove a Bob from the gel list and the RastPort . SYNOPSIS RemIBob(bob, rp, vp) A0 A1 A2 void RemIBob(struct Bob *, struct RastPort *, struct ViewPort *); FUNCTION Removes a Bob immediately by uncoupling it from the gel list and erases it from the RastPort .
