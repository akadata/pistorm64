TITLE: graphics.library/AndRectRegion

graphics.library/AndRectRegion ]" border=0> NAME AndRectRegion -- Perform 2d AND operation of rectangle with region, leaving result in region. SYNOPSIS AndRectRegion(region,rectangle) a0 a1 void AndRectRegion( struct Region *, struct Rectangle * ); FUNCTION Clip away any portion of the region that exists outside of the rectangle. Leave the result in region. INPUTS region - pointer to Region structure rectangle - pointer to Rectangle structure NOTES Unlike the other rect-region primitives, AndRectRegion() cannot fail.
