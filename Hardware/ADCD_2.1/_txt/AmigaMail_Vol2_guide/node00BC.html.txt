IV-77: Optimized Window Refreshing ]" border=0> by Martin Taillefer Maintaining the graphical contents of an Amiga window can be difficult. There are many subtleties associated with the process known as window refreshing. At present, many applications refresh their windows in suboptimal ways, or fail to refresh correctly under all conditions. This article attempts to explore and resolve the window refreshing problems commonly encountered by applications.
