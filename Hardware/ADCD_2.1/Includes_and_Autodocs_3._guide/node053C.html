NAME
	UnlockRealTime -- unlock internal lists. (V37)

   SYNOPSIS
	UnlockRealTime(lockHandle);
	               A0

	VOID UnlockRealTime(APTR);

   FUNCTION
	Undoes the effects of LockRealTime().

   INPUTS
	lockHandle - value returned by LockRealTime(). May be NULL.

   SEE ALSO
	LockRealTime()
