33 / / Other Chunk Management Functions / StopOnExit() ]" border=0> Whereas StopChunk() will stop the parser just as it enters the declared chunk, StopOnExit() will stop just before it leaves the chunk. This is useful for finding the end of FORM s, which would indicate that you've collected all possible data in this FORM and may now act on it. /* Ask ParseIFF() to stop with IFFERR_EOC when leaving a FORM ILBM */ StopOnExit(iff,ID_ILBM, ID_FORM); [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
