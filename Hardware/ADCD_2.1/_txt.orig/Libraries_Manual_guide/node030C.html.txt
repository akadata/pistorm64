A1 Points to the data area specified by the is_Data field of the Interrupt structure. Because this pointer is always fetched (regardless of whether you use it), it is to your advantage to make some use of it (scratch). A5 Points to your interrupt code (scratch). A6 Scratch. In a server chain, the interrupt is cleared automatically by the system. Having a server clear its interrupt is not recommended and not necessary (clearing could cause the loss of an interrupt on PORTS or EXTER ).
