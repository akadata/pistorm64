11 Special Functions / Easy Memory Allocation and Deallocation ]" border=0> Intuition has a pair of routines that enable applications to make multiple memory allocations which are easily deallocated with a single call. The Intuition routines for memory management are AllocRemember() and FreeRemember() . These routines rely upon the Remember structure to track allocations.
