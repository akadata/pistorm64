30 / Layers / Types of Layers ]" border=0> The layers library supports three types of layers: simple refresh, smart refresh and super bitmap. The type of the layer, specified by the Flags field in the Layer s structure, determines what facilities the layer provides. Use Only One Layer Type Flag ---------------------------- The three layer-type Flags are mutually exclusive. That is, only one layer-type flag (LAYERSIMPLE, LAYERSMART and LAYERSUPER) should be specified.
