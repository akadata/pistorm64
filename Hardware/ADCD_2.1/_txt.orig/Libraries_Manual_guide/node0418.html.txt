32 Expansion Library / Expansion Board Drivers ]" border=0> The Amiga operating system contains support for matching up disk-based drivers with AUTOCONFIG boards. Though such drivers are commonly Exec devices, this is not required. The driver may, for instance, be an Exec library or task. Since 1.3, the system software also supports the initialization of onboard ROM driver software.
