Glossary / hold-and-modify (HAM) ]" border=0> A display mode that gives you extended color selection. Normally, the Amiga supports up to 32 different colors from a palette of 4,096. Hold-and-modify (HAM mode) allows all 4,096 colors on the screen at one time by placing some restrictions on which colors may be displayed near each other. [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
