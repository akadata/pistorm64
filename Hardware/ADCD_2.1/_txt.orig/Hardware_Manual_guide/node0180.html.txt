8 / / Reading Mouse/Trackball Controllers / Reading the Counters ]" border=0> The mouse/trackball counter contents can be accessed by reading register addresses named JOY0DAT and JOY1DAT . These registers contain counts for ports 1 and 2 respectively. The contents of each of these 16-bit registers are as follows: Bits 15-8 Mouse/trackball vertical count Bits 7-0 Mouse/trackball horizontal count [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
