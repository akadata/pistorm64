IV-17: Opening Screens and Windows on Any Amiga ]" border=0> by Ewout Walraven The release 2.0 intuition.library has a multitude of new display possibilities at its disposal that developers can use to improve their software. Many products can benefit from the flexibility that higher display resolutions and larger display areas offer and from the polished user interface available with the "New Look" in release 2.0. Because backward compatibility with 1.3 is important to many developers, these new features should be used in a way that will work with older systems. This article shows how to take advantage of the display resources available to Intuition under any Amiga system. This article assumes the reader has some knowledge of release 2.0 (V36) screens and windows. For more information, see the article " An Introduction to V36 Screens and Windows " from the September/October 1990 issue of Amiga Mail. Opening Full Size Screens PAL or NTSC?
