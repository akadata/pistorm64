AIFF.doc / AIFF / The Comments Chunk ]" border=0> The Comments Chunk is used to store comments in the FORM AIFF. "EA IFF 85" has an Annotation Chunk (used in ASIF) that can be used for comments, but the Comments Chunk has two features not found in the "EA IFF 85" chunk. They are a time-stamp for the comment and a link to a marker. Comment Comments Chunk Format [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
