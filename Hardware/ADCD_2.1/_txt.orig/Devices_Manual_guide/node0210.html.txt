ACBM.doc / FORM ]" border=0> FORM ID: ACBM (Amiga Contiguous BitMap) FORM Description: FORM ACBM has the same format as FORM ILBM except the normal BODY chunk (InterLeaved BitMap) is replaced by an ABIT chunk (Amiga BITplanes). FORM Purpose: To enable faster loading/saving of screens, especially from Basic, while retaining the flexibility and portability of IFF format files. [Back to Amiga Developer Docs ]
