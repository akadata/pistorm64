iffparse.library/StopChunks ]" border=0> NAME StopChunks -- Declare many stop chunks at once. SYNOPSIS error = StopChunks (iff, list, n) d0 a0 a1 d0 LONG error; struct IFFHandle *iff; LONG *list; LONG n; FUNCTION (is to StopChunk() as PropChunks() is to PropChunk().)
