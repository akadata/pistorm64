mathieeesingbas.library/IEEESPDiv ]" border=0> NAME IEEESPDiv -- divide one single precision IEEE by another SYNOPSIS x = IEEESPDiv( y , z ); d0 d0 d1 float x,y,z; FUNCTION Compute x = y / z in IEEE single precision. Note that the Motorola fast floating point Div routine reverses the order of the arguments for the C interface, although the dividend is still in d0 and the divisor is in d1.
