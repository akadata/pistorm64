clipboard.device/CBD_CURRENTWRITEID ]" border=0> NAME CBD_CURRENTWRITEID -- Determine the current write identifier. FUNCTION CBD_CURRENTWRITEID fills the io_ClipID with a clip identifier that can be compared with that of a post command: if greater than the post identifier then the post is obsolete and need never be satisfied.
