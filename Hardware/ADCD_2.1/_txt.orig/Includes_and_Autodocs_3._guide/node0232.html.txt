exec.library/RemSemaphore ]" border=0> NAME RemSemaphore -- remove a signal semaphore from the system SYNOPSIS RemSemaphore(signalSemaphore) A1 void RemSemaphore(struct SignalSemaphore *); FUNCTION This function removes a signal semaphore structure from the system's signal semaphore list. Subsequent attempts to rendezvous by name with this semaphore will fail.
