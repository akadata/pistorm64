Gadgets may include text information in the form of a linked list of
IntuiText structures.  A pointer to the first IntuiText structure in the
list is placed in the Gadget structure's GadgetText field.  The text is
positioned relative to the top left corner of the gadget's select box.
For more information on IntuiText, see the
"Intuition Images, Line Drawing and Text" chapter.
