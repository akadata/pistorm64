    (Home Position)                      <CSI>H  or          9B 48

                                         <CSI>;1H or         9B 3B 31 48
                                         <CSI>1;H            9B 31 3B 48

                                         <CSI>;4H            9B 3B 34 48

    Clear the window:                    <FF> or             0C

    (home and clear to end of window)    <CSI>H<CSI>J        9B 48 9B 4A
